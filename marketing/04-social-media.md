# Social Media — Detailed Plan

> TikTok first (algorithmic reach for new accounts), Instagram Reels second (cross-posted via N8N).

---

## 4 Content Pillars

| Pillar | % | Description |
|--------|---|-------------|
| Menu Expose | 40% | Film at restaurant → scan menu → reveal surprising scores |
| Health Hacks | 25% | Educational tips: "3 things to look for on any menu" |
| Build in Public | 20% | Solo founder story, dev journey, behind the scenes |
| User Reactions | 15% | Friends trying the app, reactions to scores |

---

## "Menu Expose" Video Formula

1. **Hook (0-2s):** "This restaurant has a DIRTY SECRET on their menu..."
2. **Body (2-15s):** Show scan happening, results loading, surprising scores
3. **Payoff (15-20s):** "The [dish] scored a 94. Here's why..."
4. **CTA (20-25s):** "Link in bio to try it yourself"

---

## Proven Viral Video Ideas

- **"I scanned the entire McDonald's menu"** — chain restaurants = massive search volume
- **"The 'healthy' salad that scored LOWER than the burger"** — subverted expectations = shares
- **"Rating my date's restaurant choice"** — relationship angle = engagement
- **"My nutritionist was WRONG about this dish"** — authority challenge = controversy
- **"POV: Your friend scans the menu before you order"** — relatable humor
- **"I only ate 90+ scored dishes for a week"** — challenge format = series potential
- **"Which fast food chain has the healthiest menu?"** — comparison = debate in comments

---

## Solo UGC Approach (no budget needed)

**Equipment:**
- Phone camera (front cam for talking, rear for food/menu)
- Cheap phone tripod ($10-15) or lean against something
- Natural lighting (sit near window at restaurants)

**Screen recording:** Android built-in screen recorder for app demos

**Batch filming:** Record 3-4 restaurant visits in one week, edit/schedule over 2-3 weeks

---

## Tools
- **CapCut** (free) — editing + auto-captions (critical: 80% watch on mute)
- **Android screen recorder** — app demo footage
- **N8N** — handles all distribution after you upload to TikTok
