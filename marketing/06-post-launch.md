# Post-Launch Growth — Detailed Plan

> First 30 days after store approval.

---

## Content

### Chain Restaurant Expose Videos (trend magnets)
These get massive search volume. Film yourself scanning the full menu at:
- McDonald's
- Chipotle
- Olive Garden
- Panera Bread
- Chili's
- Taco Bell

Format: "I scanned the ENTIRE [chain] menu — here are the results"

### Micro-Influencer Outreach
- Target: fitness/nutrition TikTokers, 10K-50K followers
- Offer: free premium access in exchange for one honest review video
- Find them: search TikTok for "healthy eating", "meal prep", "fitness food"
- Reach out to 5-10 in the first 2 weeks
- Template DM: "Hey [name], built an app that scans restaurant menus for health scores. Would love for you to try it — I'll give you free premium. No strings, just want your honest take."

## In-App Growth Features

### Comparison Mode (Phase 2 viral mechanic)
- Select 2 restaurants from scan history
- Side-by-side health score comparison card
- Shareable: "Chipotle vs Sweetgreen — which is actually healthier?"
- New screen: `app/(main)/compare.tsx`

### Personal Stats Dashboard
- Weekly health score trend graph
- Best finds this week
- Streak tracking (days of scanning)
- Shareable: "My clean eating week" — accountability posts
- New screen: `app/(main)/stats.tsx`
